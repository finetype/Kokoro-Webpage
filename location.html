Location

We hold classes at Givens Recreational Center, in east Austin, near 12th and Airport.

Schedule

Classes are Tuesday & Thursday @ 6:30pm, and Saturday @ Noon. All classes are 1.5 hours long.

Classes may be cancelled in the event of holidays or extreme weather; class cancellations will be announced in the facebook group. (It is a closed group to prevent spam, but anyone is welcome to request to join the group and will be accepted.)

You can also contact Givens Recration Center directly to see if the building itself is closed during inclement weather or various holidays.
